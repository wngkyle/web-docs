Unit Conversion
Temperature



To convert celcius to fahrenheit yourself, use this formula replacing the 'c' with your temperature in celcius: (C * 9/5) + 32
Weight



To convert kilograms to pounds yourself, use this formula replacing the 'kg' with your weight in kilograms: kg * 2.205
Distance



To convert kilometer to miles yourself, use this formula replacing the 'km' with your distance in kilometer: km ÷ 1.609
